MoveKit

Your Personal Home Gym Trainer

MoveKit is a set of home gym products including an AI-powered mirror with a mobile app to train and record users' workout performance

/ Client Brief

'Let's keep people moving during the pandemic lockdown.'

The client has come to us with a very detailed plan already. Our role is to organise and clarify everything she needs to achieve her goal

/ Tasks

How trainer's video should be recorded

As the trainers' video will be processed with AI alongside other configurations from the cms. Guidance on how videos should be recorded is needed

Monitoring all visual materials

To maintain a consistent tone across the branding, the UI, the videos and the image assets. We have to participate in all shooting and post-editing processes

UIUX – Mirror to Mobile to Watch

The project contains apps collaboration across devices. Testing and making sure a logical UIUX across devices and use case is required

CMS Logic

CMS structure on how to and what to configure to the front-end is responsible by us. Prioritizing configurable features within the time limit and budget is needed

/ The Design

UI Overview

Neon green with bold typography treatment to make it visually exciting and sporty. Monotone image with motion gesture to keep things hyped
