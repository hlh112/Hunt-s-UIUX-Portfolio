MANTRA DAO

We Grow Wealth Together

MANTRA DAO is a crypto startup, like many others, being suspected as scam all the time. The DAO wants to gain reputation by improving through UIUX

/ Client Brief

'We are not scammers lol.'

There are many reasons for being accused as scammers, UIUX is certainly one. The company never hire any UIUX role to pick up the job resulting in loads of doubt stemmed solely from the app interface

/ Pain Points

The Insecure Nature in Crypto Space

Crypto is still relative new to many. Trading on DeFi usually requires steps that are not found in Trad-Fi. Unfamiliar processes with poor UX make users feel insecure and prompt to error

Preliminary UIUX design

Theres never a UIUX designer in the team before. The original interface is created to mainly test the market. Thus, huge potential for improvements on UIUX

/ Strategies

Define Target Audience

With no clear definition of our primary audience, its hard to draw a line on making UIUX decision when it comes to problems that everyone feels different way

Enhance Respect & Empathy

Know the pain points of our users, where in the app that most of them will get lost and confused. Do not just stand at the veiw of a more experienced investors

Don’t reinvent the wheel

Follows what the lead players in the space have been practicing. Make users feel secure and familiar

/ Solution

New Re-Designed UI for Layman

The team studied the comments received from daily interactions with the community members. Targeting the plain points that were usually raised and adjust accordingly

/ The Design

UI Overview


👇 tap on the UI to to explore



A More Error-Proofing UI

To secure users’ wealth, we always ask for confirmation on any transaction in the newly designed UI. An inline state-chainging approach is applied on the button components to avoid miss-clicking and keep the UI neat



A More Secured User Experience

Making transactions with any asset for the first time, users will have to approve the deal before making the actual transfer. Newbies are confused thinking that transfer is done at the first step. Here we created a more self-explained UI to reduce confusion. The whole point is to keep every steps all at a glance and be slightly ‘redundant’ in content writing



UI with Constant Feedbacks

To avoid miss-clicking, the UI always responds to users’ actions or states. Taking the below example, the button will be disabled if the conditions are not fully met with inline complimentary text guiding the users



Learn Together

We treasure every chance to better equip our users. Here we take the long pending time of validating a blockchain transaction as a chance to educate our users with useful crypto 101 info



No Longer a Newbie?

UI is unavoidably more clumsy with more complimentary controls which maybe unfriendly to experienced users. That’s why most of the above treatment is bundled in a mode that can be toggled
