HKTDC Marketplace

The All-in-one HKTDC Passbook

The HK based council that brings business opportunities for local companies via holding commercial exhibitions wants to make their visiting experience more seamless

/ Client Brief

'The congestion problem at the entrance is a pain in the arse.'

As the staff was told from survey that visiting experience to their exhibition hall is painfully lag especially during check-in. The council members want a fundamental revamp of the journey

/ Pain Points

Congestion at entrance

The check-in process is done manually with physical tickets. Visitors often find themselves waiting in long queue before they got admited

Low app conversion rate

There is an existing app for obtaining exhibition news, but people barely use it due to its overly simple usage. People rather read the printed brochure instead

/ Insights

'How about an All-in-one digital passbook?'

As the client wants to pump the app conversion rate while willing to have a fundamental revamping. We decided to digitalise and integrate the check-in process into the app along side with other new features

/ Strategies

NFC check-in

Instead of using physical tickets and having the visitors admited manually one by one. We decided to use nfc for admission with less man-power required

Value Adding

The app should serve not just as a read only app, but should help the visitors to expand their business network. New features like e-Business card, business matching is added
