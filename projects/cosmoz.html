A Dating App Empowered by Feng Shui

The Feng Shui Consultation Company 'Forseesers' is now seeking a way to digitalise their business with an innovative approach

/ Client Brief

'Find a way to digitalise & sell our existing products.'

The Company itself has a solid history in providing Feng Shui (Chinese fortuning-telling) services. It now wants to figure a way to digitalise its physical products to scale the sales

/ Existing Products & Services

The 'Lifebook'

A yearly fortune telling publication that forecasts one's luck in the coming year

Jewels & Accessories

Wearables that help enhance one's luck of certain aspects e.g. career & romance

/ Insights

'Another eCommerce platform? Hm... probably not.'

The client's initial idea was to have a new eCommerce site integrating all their available services & products to drive online sales. But...

/ The Problems

Repeating a similar existing execution

The client already has those products on their corporate site integrated with a payment gateway

No new audience is attracted

An eCommerce platform only converts the existing offline audience online. No new audience is earned

/ The Solution

A Dating App empowered by a Feng Shui Algorithm

/ Reasonings

Expanding target audience

While it is hard to alter existing customers' behaviour, a new digital solution serves to expand the audience base to the younger generation

Add value to existing products

Instead of selling the 'Lifebook' directly, its content is integrated seamlessly into the app to create a new dating experience

/ The Design

UI Overview

The overall tonality is set to be mystic with graphics & colour palette mimicking stars in the night – the origin of most of the Chinese Feng Shui philosophy



👈 tap on the phone to explore

/ Feature 1

Your Portable Fortune Teller

While not everyone is eager to meet new friends,the app will primarily provide personal fortune telling at the landing screen based on the profile info provided

👉 Free Daily Fortuning Telling

Base on the user's birth date & time, the app will tell how their luck would look in four aspects

👉 Unlock More with Tokens

The app is designed with in-app tokens. Users can reviewmore content by spending the tokens

/ Feature 2

Meet Friends with the app's Feng Shui Algorithm

The dating algorithm will match users with others based on: 1. Zodiac Constellations / 2. Horoscope / 3. Chinese Numerology

👉 Discover New Matches

Recommended matches based on how well the zodiac and horoscope are matched to users

👉 Checkout Their Profile

Take a loook at the matches profile andsee how the 'Lifebook' describes about them

👉 See How Well The Match is

Users will be told about how well the matching is with detailed explanations and also cautions that have to be aware of

👉 The Accessories Integration

To better avoid the cautions in a matched relationship, users can take a look at the wearables to enhance theircharm and luck

/ Feature 3

the 'Lifebook' integration

The 'Lifebook' is a yearly publication that contains a whole lot of questions that forecasts one's life. Instead of an once and for all purchase of the whole physical book, questions & chapters inside are used as atom units for users to unlock one by one

/ Feature 4

Gamification

In order to ensure converstion rate, a token system is applied with missions and rewards being delivered on a regular base. Users can unlock more features & contents just like playing any digital games


Missions & Rewards

👉 Quick Chat
