Keldi

A Community for Shopaholics

A social platform designed specifically for people to ask, review and share on the products they bought or want to purchase

/ Client Brief

'I want to create a twitter, but specifically for honest product review.'

The client has come to us for a MVP on creating a social platform specifically made for peer review on the purchases they made

/ Pain Points

Clumsy onboarding

Users have to import product by product on what they purchased in reality into the app. It is rather time consuming and may defect conversion rate

Trust issue

Review platforms are often flood with scams and paid comments. How to gain reputation for honesty and reliability will be a big how

/ Insights

'More people do online shopping these days right?'

To tackle the problem of clumsy content creation process in app, this insight leads us to integration with people's online shopping record to create instant content for the app

/ Strategies

Content is King

Keep the app simple and minimal. Focus primarily on 'Product Review' and to ensure content's quality in gaining reputation

Fast Onboarding

Instead of having users to import product by product into the app in a clumsy manner, we try to automate the process

/ Solution to clumsy content creation

Mail Inbox Integration for quick data import

In order to make data import less clumsy, the app will ask user permission to scan through their inbox for any recipt / invoice emails. The app will then crawl the specific data from those emails for fast onboarding



/ Solution to content quality

A more complex user evaluation mechanism

Instead of having a simple upvote vs downvote mechanism to determine a post popularity. The app will analyse the user's profile as a whole to determine whether to push posts from that user to the community

/ Feature 1

Post Types

The app provides three types of post for users to create whatever content they want to publish about a product

👉 Product Review

For user to review a product. One can state whether she is a tester or owner of the product and if it is recommended or not

👉 Poll Post

Offer users to ask question in form of poll

👉 Plain Text

Offer users to start a pure discussion on a product

/ Feature 2

Share / Ask For Opinions?

The app classifies users into Buyers and Reviewers by limiting the post types to generate content

/ Feature 3
