Leighton CPCS

Steamlined Workflow for the Company

The company is now seeking a more organised way to manage their workflow throughtout a construction project and to reduce mistakes

/ Client Brief

'No more "paper" work...'

The company has been suffered from the high monitoring cost throughout every steps closing a construction project. The company thinks that automation is the way out

/ Pain Points

Too Physical

Throughout the construction project, tones of paperwork has to be filled by different parties. Most of these processes are done using physical paper which is time consuming and is more subject to mistakes and losts

Too Scatered

Lost in information is common since a project typically contains several contractors. Different contractors may have their own way in managing and completing the same task. Variation in practice results in high cost of supervising

/ Solution

Single Source of Truth

A centralised portal is created to manage everything needed throught the entire project lifespan

/ Strategies

Form Builder – Unified Format

A form builder is developed for the company admin to build forms required in the entire project in a unified format

Small Apps – Digitalise Every Steps

An app is created for every critical steps occured during the entire project lifespan to make paperwork more accessible
