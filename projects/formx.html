/ Features Overview

Continuously Evolving...

The team has been dedicated to delivering cutting-edge technology, and with the emergence of LLM models in 2023, our focus has been on integrating LLM into the platform to provide users with a state-of-the-art experience

/ 01

Progressive Onboarding

Aiming to impress potential users with a hands-on UX that is user-friendly, even for non-tech users, creating memorable "Aha" moments.

/ 02

Few-Shot Learning Extraction

Integrating LLM models to offer a seamless data extraction experience with minimal setup and training effort.

/ 03

LLM Schema Generator

A hassle-free experience that saves massive setup time in the preparation stage of extraction.

/ 04

Post-Processing Formatter

To further adjust the extracted data to fine-tune accuracy and format for custom business needs.

/ 05

Validation Engine

Automates logic validation on extracted data, enabling further integration into downstream business systems.
